The central theme of Windlight Island is restoration and harmony with nature. Instead of focusing on conflict or survival against threats, the game explores how peace, patience, and curiosity can heal a world that has fallen out of balance. The island itself acts as a living character once vibrant but now quiet and incomplete until the player restores its lost energy.
